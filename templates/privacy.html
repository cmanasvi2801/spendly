Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly ("we", "our", "the Service") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you use the Spendly personal expense tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect:

  • Your email address and password (stored securely) used to sign in
  • Expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions
  • Basic usage information needed to operate the Service, such as login timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to operate and improve the Service — for example, to authenticate your account, display your expense history, and generate spending summaries. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in the Service's database and protected with reasonable technical and administrative safeguards. While we work to protect your information, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the contact details provided within the Service.

5. Cookies

Spendly may use session cookies to keep you signed in. These cookies are necessary for the Service to function and are not used for advertising or tracking across other sites.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the Service or applicable law. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact details provided within the Service.
